About Our CBO

We source funds from: grants, well-wishers, contributions from Child Development Centers stakeholders and yearly fund drive.

Approximately 1,500 children have benefited from the project by receiving 70% of their semester/term fees. We have managed to maintain many students in schools hence, school dropout has significantly reduced with 30% especially in Kilifi County. From the benefitting team, we are proud to have a good number of graduates from universities and college. The project has transformed the negative attitude towards Education in the community into a positive perspective, thus many parents have embraced learning
